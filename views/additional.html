ADDITIONAL SERVICES

Interior Car Detailing- We will remove all pet hair, vacuum out the carpets and seats, treat the interior plastic, and clean the windows. The cost of this service starts at $30.00 and will increase depending on how long it takes to clean.

Pet Supply Delivery- We will pick up any supplies you need for your pets and deliver them to your door. The cost is whatever the supplies cost plus an additional $5.

Dog Walking/Play Time- We will come to your house and take your dogs for a walk or play in the yard with them for at least 15 minutes. The cost for this service starts at $5 a visit depending on what you choose for the play time.
