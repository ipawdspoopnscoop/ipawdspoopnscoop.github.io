Staff Members

SHYANN HADERLIE

Shyann Haderlie is also a joint onwer at Ipawd's Poop 'N Scoop LLC. Shyann helped Donna start the business back in 2011 and has been very involved with the growth of the company. You will still see her at events and possibly out picking up your yard. She is a Wyoming native and grew up around animals. Her family owned a ranch which was responsible for taking care of more than 40 head of horses, dogs, cats and birds. Shyann is a huge animal lover and animal rights activist. Shyann and Donna have three dogs two cats and two parakeets at this time.

Just like Shyann you will still see Donna out at events and once in awhile cleaning your yard, but the main face of the company will be Garrett Holmes. Donna is a Pocatello native and has found a deep love for animals with the help of Shyann. Together with Shyann, Donna does what she can to be proactive in helping animals in any way possible.
