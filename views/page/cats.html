KITTY SERVICES

We love and care about all pets and so we also offer services for our feline friends!

Litter box services can be purchased with a regular yard cleaning service for a discount or on its own for the full price.

Litter box services include sifting and/or emptying. When being emptied we will wipe down, sanitize and refill the litter boxes. When sifting we will remove all waste and top it off. The client will supply all kitty litter and sifters. Additional litter boxes, sifters, and kitty litter can be purchased and delivered by us for an additional fee.

Occurrence Cost
With a weekly yard cleaning $7.00/month per litter box
Without a weekly yard cleaning $14.00/month per litter box
